About PhishEye

PhishEye is a URL scanning tool designed to help you identify potentially malicious or phishing websites. It uses a Machine Learning model to analyze various features of a URL and predict whether it's safe, suspicious, or dangerous.

Our goal is to provide a quick and easy way to check URLs before you click, helping to protect you from online threats.

Always exercise caution when browsing the internet, even with tools like PhishEye.
